## Key Concepts to Track Across Readings

As you read, track how these concepts appear:

1. **Microworlds** (Papert)
   - "Insulated and accessible islands of activity"
   - Low floor, high ceiling
   - Transitional systems

2. **Zone of Proximal Development** (Vygotsky)
   - Actual vs potential development
   - Mediation through tools/signs
   - Guided participation

3. **Genetic Epistemology** (Piaget)
   - Assimilation and accommodation
   - Stages of development
   - Construction of knowledge

4. **Transitional Objects** (Winnicott)
   - Between subjective and objective reality
   - Role in separation and development
   - Potential space for play

5. **Constructionism** (Papert)
   - Learning through making
   - Public entities
   - Concrete thinking vs abstract

6. **Intellectual Need** (Harel)
   - Necessity principle
   - Problem-laden vs problem-free activity
   - Epistemological need vs motivation
